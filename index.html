This site holds a collection of street photography by Anna Truong.

Anna found her interest in photography when she was looking for a break in life. Wondering around different places and observing the surroundings provided a different perspective on situations and promoted a sense of ‘being in the moment’. It has now become an ongoing hobby and a part of mindfulness practice.
